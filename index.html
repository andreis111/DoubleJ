Communal Cleaning

Including:

  • Entry Door wiping to ensure free of dirt, dust, fingerprints or smears.
  • Foyer Mopping at every visit to ensure that it is free of dust, spillages or dirt.
  • Lift Mopping and Wiping to ensure that it is free of litter, dust, dirt or spillages.
  • Flooring Vacuum cleaning including edges and skirtings shall be cleaned in such a way to preserve floor covering. The process should ensure all floor areas are reasonably clear of debris or dust.
  • Internal glass surfaces wiping to include surrounds, ledges, paintwork and fittings.
  • Stairs vacuum to ensure all stairs are clear of debris or dust.
  • Wiping of handrails to ensure free of dust or dirt.
  • Internal/Communal doors wiping to be free of dirt, dust, fingerprints or smears.
  • Dusting ceiling surfaces from cobwebs and other debris.
  • Garage Litter picking and sweeping to be maintained in a clean and litter free condition avoiding any build-up of dirt, soiling, litter, leaves or other debris.
  • Bin Compound Sweeping at every visit.
